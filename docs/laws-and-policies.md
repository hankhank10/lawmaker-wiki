# Laws & Policies

This is the reference for the **political issues** that define your platform and the **laws** you'll propose and vote on.

## Political issues

There are **16 political issues** in Lawmaker. When you create a party you choose **4** of them as your [pillars](gameplay/parties.md#your-ideology-the-4-pillars), setting a position on each. Every issue runs on a spectrum from **-1** to **+1** (with 0 being neutral), and your positions are what [voters](gameplay/elections.md#understanding-electors) compare your record against.

The first eleven issues are two-sided spectrums:

| Issue | -1 | +1 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 🙋‍♀️ **Gender Equality** | Traditional gender roles | Full gender equality |
| ⛪ **Role of Religion** | Secular governance | Religion in government |
| 🌱 **Environment** | Growth first | Environmental protection |
| 🗽 **Individual Liberty** | Strong leadership | Maximum personal freedom |
| ⚔️ **Military** | Diplomatic solutions | Military strength |
| 🌍 **Immigration** | Border security | Open borders |
| 💰 **Economy** | Wealth redistribution | Free market |
| 🎭 **Multiculturalism** | National identity | Multiculturalism |
| 👮‍♂️ **Law and Order** | Rehabilitation | Strict enforcement |
| 🔨 **Workers' Rights** | Business growth | Worker protections |
| 🛡️ **Welfare State** | Sink or swim | Welfare programs |

The remaining five are **single-position** issues — a party either champions them or doesn't:

| Issue | Champions |
| --- | --- |
| 🏗️ **Infrastructure Investment** | Roads, bridges, and utilities |
| 📚 **Education Investment** | Schools and universities |
| 👶 **Support for Young People** | Education, housing, opportunity for the young |
| 👨‍💼 **Support for Middle-Aged People** | Working families, careers, work–life balance |
| 👴 **Support for Older People** | Healthcare, pensions, quality of life in old age |

Pick four issues that form a coherent identity, and be ready to vote for them consistently — that's what builds a recognisable brand with voters.

## The laws catalogue

There are **95 laws** across 21 policy areas — defence, the economy, education, environment, healthcare, immigration, and more. Each law has several options representing different positions, and a [proposal](gameplay/legislation.md) changes one or more laws from their current option to a new one.

The full, authoritative catalogue — with each law's current setting, its available options, and their political impacts — is shown **in the game itself** when you draft a proposal, and on each country's **Laws** page. That's always the up-to-date source, so refer there rather than to a static list.
