# Laws & Policies

This comprehensive guide covers all **political issues** and **laws** available in Lawmaker. Understanding these helps you define your party's ideology and propose effective legislation.

## Political Issues

Your party takes positions on **16 political issues**. These positions define your ideology and affect how [voters](gameplay/elections.md#understanding-voters) perceive you.

### Issue Spectrum

Each issue has a spectrum from **-1 to +1**:

- **-1.0** = Strong position toward one extreme
- **0.0** = Neutral/centrist position
- **+1.0** = Strong position toward opposite extreme

## Using This Information

### For Party Creation

When [creating your party](gameplay/parties.md):

1. **Choose 8-10 priority issues** from the 16 available
2. **Set your positions** on each (-1 to +1 scale)
3. **Build coherent ideology** - Make sure positions align
4. **Consider voter appeal** - What will electors support?

### For Proposing Laws

When [proposing legislation](gameplay/legislation.md):

1. **Review current state** - What's the status quo?
2. **Choose target option** - Which option do you want?
3. **Check issue impacts** - How does this affect political issues?
4. **Predict voter response** - Will voters reward this vote?

### For Strategic Voting

When [voting on proposals](gameplay/legislation.md#voting-on-proposals):

1. **Check issue impacts** - Does this align with your ideology?
2. **Consider electoral effects** - How will voters see this vote?
3. **Review your pillars** - Is this consistent with your party?
4. **Think long-term** - Build coherent legislative record
