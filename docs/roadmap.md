## Planned Features

### Short-Term (Next 3-6 Months)

#### Enhanced Voter Simulation

**Status:** In Planning

**Description:** Make voters more sophisticated and responsive

- **Dynamic voter preferences** - Voters' positions shift over time
- **Issue salience** - Some issues matter more during different periods
- **Voter segments** - Clear demographic groups (urban/rural, young/old, etc.)
- **Swing voters** - Undecided voters who can be persuaded
- **Turnout modeling** - Better simulation of who votes and why

**Impact:** More realistic and engaging electoral competition

---

#### Legislative Improvements

**Status:** Under Development

**Description:** Enhance the proposal and voting system

- **Amendments** - Allow proposing changes to active proposals
- **Committee system** - Pre-vote discussion and refinement
- **Proposal priorities** - Mark important proposals
- **Vote explanations** - Require brief reason for votes
- **Legislative agenda** - Parties declare upcoming proposals

**Impact:** Richer legislative gameplay and strategy

---

#### Advanced Analytics

**Status:** Concept Phase

**Description:** Better tools for understanding your position

- **Detailed voter breakdowns** - See which voter segments support you
- **Vote impact analysis** - "If you voted Yes, you'd gain/lose X votes"
- **Coalition calculator** - Test cabinet distributions before proposing
- **Trend tracking** - Historical charts of vote share over time
- **Comparative analysis** - How your party compares to rivals

**Impact:** More strategic decision-making

---

### Medium-Term (6-12 Months)

## Development Philosophy

### Core Principles

Our development follows these principles:

1. **Player-Driven** - Features based on player needs and feedback
2. **Depth over Breadth** - Deep mechanics better than shallow features
3. **Strategic Focus** - Everything should enable strategy
4. **Accessibility** - Complex doesn't mean complicated
5. **Balance** - No dominant strategy or pay-to-win

### What We Won't Add

Some things don't fit Lawmaker's vision:

- âœ— **Direct player conflict** - No attacking other parties' resources
- âœ— **Pay-to-win mechanics** - No purchasing PP or votes
- âœ— **Real-world politics** - Game remains fictional
- âœ— **Random chance** - Minimize RNG in favor of strategy
- âœ— **Time pressure** - Game pace remains relaxed
